# Epiow — voice & samples

## Voice in one line

**Composed, precise, bilingual-capable. Enterprise-serious without bureaucracy cosplay.**

## Principles

1. **Say the operating truth.** Prefer “leave approval” over “empower your workforce journey.”  
2. **Earn trust with structure.** Lists, clear nouns, no fog.  
3. **Local when it matters.** Name HK law and MPF when relevant; don’t fetishize geography.  
4. **AI is a verb, not a costume.** Describe what it does inside the OS.  
5. **Never over-claim compliance.** We build paths and workflows; legal outcomes need counsel.

## Tone slider

| Too cold | On brand | Too hype |
|----------|----------|----------|
| “Pursuant to module 4.2…” | “Approve leave in one flow.” | “Revolutionize HR with AI!!” |
| Feature dump only | Problem → system → module | Lifestyle startup vibes |

## Sample — product update (EN)

We’ve shipped modular leave entitlements with Hong Kong statutory patterns as first-class options. The point isn’t another settings screen — it’s fewer edge cases at month-end. Payroll paths that touch MPF remain explicit and reviewable before anything posts.

## Sample — product update (繁中)

我們上線了以香港法定假期模式為一等選項的模組化假期權益。重點不是多一頁設定，而是月結時少一點例外。觸及強積金的出糧路徑會保持明確，並在入帳前可覆核。

## Sample — error / empty state (EN)

No apps in your catalog yet. Start with People or connect your first module from the platform catalog.

## Sample — sales email open (EN)

Subject: One workspace for HK people ops + the rest of the org  

Body: Most teams we meet run leave, attendance, and payroll across three tools and a spreadsheet. Epiow is the organization OS underneath — modular apps, one login, HK-native people workflows. Happy to show a 20-minute path from catalog to first approval flow.

## Banned phrases (unless legal/product explicitly signed)

- “100% compliant” / “fully compliant with all HK law”  
- “Replace your entire ERP tomorrow”  
- “AGI for HR”  
- “Bank-grade” without infrastructure evidence
