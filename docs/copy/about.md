## Full About — EN

### Who we are

Epiow Limited builds **Epiow** — an enterprise workspace designed as an **organization operating system**. Not another chat tab. Not a payroll island. A platform where apps for people, customers, and operations share one identity, one catalog, and one operational truth.

We focus first on Hong Kong organizations: teams that cannot treat local labour law and MPF as optional configuration. Global suites often ship locale packs. Spreadsheets and messaging fill the gaps. Epiow is built so those rules live in the product’s spine.

### Why we exist

Organizations fragment as they grow. Leave lives in one tool. Attendance in another. Payroll in a third. CRM somewhere else. AI is then asked to “understand the business” with no coherent system underneath.

Epiow exists to reverse that pattern: **one OS for the organization**, with modular apps that stay isolated where they should and connected where they must.

### What we believe

1. **Clarity beats theatre.** Enterprise software should reduce ambiguity, not add dashboards for their own sake.  
2. **Modules beat monoliths — when the platform is real.** Apps should be catalog-backed and accountable, not a single tangled codebase pretending to be a suite.  
3. **Local law is product, not paperwork.** For Hong Kong, Employment Ordinance and MPF paths are first-class.  
4. **Intelligence is capability.** AI should cross apps with evidence, not costume the homepage.

### How we work

We ship a platform OS with a modular application catalog — HR and CareOps, CRM and ops, productivity surfaces — under a single workspace. Cross-app intelligence is a layer on top of structured access, not a chatbot stapled to chaos.

### Who we’re for

Founders, HR, and operations leaders in Hong Kong SMEs and mid-market organizations who need modern software **and** compliance they can defend. Implementers who want modular architecture without toy-grade foundations.

### Who we’re not for

Teams hunting for a consumer social app, a pure US-centric HR suite with a flag toggle, or an “AI wrapper” with no operational spine.

## Full About — 繁中

### 我們是誰

Epiow Limited 打造 **Epiow**——以**組織作業系統**為核心的企業工作平台。不是多一個聊天分頁，也不是孤立的出糧工具，而是人事、客戶與營運應用共享同一身份、同一目錄、同一營運真相的平台。

我們優先服務香港組織：不能把本地勞工法例與強積金當成「可選設定」的團隊。國際套件往往只給 locale pack；缺口由試算表與即時通訊填補。Epiow 從產品骨架就把這些規則做進去。

### 為何存在

組織一成長就碎片化。假期在 A、考勤在 B、出糧在 C、客戶在 D。然後有人要求 AI「理解公司」——底下卻沒有可連貫的系統。

Epiow 要扭轉這個模式：**一個組織 OS**，應用在該隔離時隔離、該連通時連通。

### 我們相信

1. **清晰勝過表演。** 企業軟件應減少歧義。  
2. **模組勝過巨石——前提是平台是真的。** 應用應可目錄化、可問責。  
3. **本地法例是產品，不是附件。** 對香港而言，《僱傭條例》與強積金路徑是一等公民。  
4. **智能是能力。** AI 應帶著結構與證據跨應用，而不是首頁裝飾。

### 給誰用

需要現代軟件、也需要站得住腳的合規的香港中小企與中型組織的負責人、人事與營運；以及要模組架構、但不要玩具地基的實作者。
